What We Offer

SMP Services

Precision scalp micropigmentation tailored to your unique needs. Every session is crafted with care.

✂️

Buzz Cut Look

Replicate the appearance of a freshly shaved head with individually crafted impressions. Perfect for complete hair loss or those who prefer a clean, confident look.

💇

Density & Thinning

Add the appearance of thickness to thinning areas. Blend seamlessly with your existing hair for a fuller, younger look without surgery and zero downtime.

🔬

Hairline Restoration

Artistically designed hairlines that complement your facial structure. Individually placed micro-impressions create a natural, undetectable result.

⭕

Alopecia Coverage

Specialized treatment for alopecia areata, totalis, and universalis. Restore a uniform appearance and reclaim your confidence.

🩹

Scar Camouflage

Conceal transplant scars, injury scars, or scalp irregularities with precise pigmentation that blends naturally into surrounding tissue.

🔄

Touch-Up & Refresh

Maintain your SMP with periodic refresh sessions. Keep your look sharp, consistent, and vibrant for years to come.

The Process

1

Free Consultation

We discuss your goals, assess your scalp, and create a custom treatment plan. No pressure, just a conversation about what SMP can do for you.

2

First Session

The foundation layer is applied. We map your hairline, match pigment to your natural tone, and begin building density with precise micro-impressions.

3

Second Session

After 7-10 days of healing, we add depth and refine the result. This session builds on the foundation to create a natural, three-dimensional look.

4

Final Touches

A third session if needed to perfect density, edges, and overall balance. You leave with a result that looks completely natural.
